Profile

ILLUSTRATOR ： KoKo Lee
Koko is a Taiwanese Canadian freelance illustrator based in Osaka, Japan.
For her illustrations, Koko combines textured calligraphy brush strokes and contemporary digital colours.
She specializes in editorial illustration for magazines, newspapers, books, and branding illustrations for stores and shops.
But she is always willing to challenge herself to work on projects for different industries.
In her spare time, she likes to solve the crimes of criminal TV dramas (before the detectives).
She also likes to daydream about creating a rival to Hello Kitty featuring her own cat, Miko.
